# Judging Checklist

## Depth of Codex Integration

Show that Codex helped with:

- scaffolding
- refactoring
- debugging
- iteration
- polish
- pitch preparation

## Real-World Impact

State:

- who the user is
- what friction exists today
- why touchless control helps
- why the problem matters beyond the demo

## Reusability and Adoption Potential

Show:

- reusable command schema
- reusable router
- multiple domain packs or future modes
- realistic packaging path such as browser extension, desktop wrapper, or internal toolkit

## Demo and Pitch Quality

Ensure the audience can see:

- the input source
- the recognized command
- the resulting action
- the value of the workflow
